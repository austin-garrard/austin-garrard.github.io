About

Speculate on which rosters pro Dota 2 players will end up on this year! Click-and-drag players to move them to and from rosters. Players that have confirmed spots on rosters have check marks and cannot be moved.

With the post-TI shuffle going on, I thought this would be a fun app to make while learning AngularJS. Let me know what you think wootasaurus@gmail.com.

Information was gathered manually and is not guaranteed to be accurate or up-to-date. Most player pictures are from StarLadder. Team logos are taken from their liquipedia entries. Background art by entroz.
